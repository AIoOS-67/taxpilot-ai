## Tax Computation Example (Single, $60,000 taxable income)

1. First $11,925 × 10% = $1,192.50
2. $11,926 to $48,475 ($36,550) × 12% = $4,386.00
3. $48,476 to $60,000 ($11,525) × 22% = $2,535.50
4. **Total Tax: $8,114.00**
5. Effective rate: 13.5%
6. Marginal rate: 22%

## Key Changes from 2024

- All bracket thresholds increased ~2.8% for inflation
- Alternative Minimum Tax (AMT) exemption: $88,100 (single), $137,000 (MFJ)
- Social Security wage base: $176,100
- Medicare additional tax: 0.9% on wages over $200,000 (single) / $250,000 (MFJ)
